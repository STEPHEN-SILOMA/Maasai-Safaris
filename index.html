Visit & enjoy at
MaraSiligi Camp

Mara Siligi Camp is a luxury safari camp located in the Maasai Mara National Reserve in Kenya.
Renowned for offering an authentic and immersive wildlife experience, the camp features well-appointed
tents that provide comfort and elegance amidst the African wilderness. Guests at Mara Siligi
Camp can enjoy activities such as game drives, guided nature walks,
and cultural interactions with the local Maasai community.
